What Happens If You Don't File On Time

ATL Surcharge

Rs. 25,000

Miss the deadline and you must pay a surcharge just to get back on the Active Taxpayers List — up from Rs. 1,000 previously.

Late Filing Penalty (Sec. 182)

0.1% per day

Daily penalty on tax payable for every day of delay, subject to minimum penalty amounts — it adds up fast.

Double Withholding Tax

Up to 2x rates

Non-filers pay far higher withholding on property transactions, vehicle registration, banking profit and dividends.

Cash Withdrawal Tax

0.8% advance tax

Non-filers are charged advance tax on daily bank cash withdrawals above Rs. 75,000 — filers pay none.

Documents by Filer Type

🧑‍💼 Salaried Individuals

  • CNIC copy (valid, not expired)
  • Salary certificate / tax deduction certificate from employer
  • Bank statement (1 July 2025 – 30 June 2026)
  • Details of assets: property, vehicle, investments
  • Utility of any other income (rent, profit on savings)

🏪 Business / AOP

  • CNIC of proprietor / partners
  • Summary of sales, purchases & expenses
  • Business bank statement (full year)
  • Utility bills of business premises
  • Details of business assets & stock

💻 Freelancers

  • CNIC copy
  • Payoneer / Wise / bank remittance record
  • Platform earning statements (Upwork, Fiverr, etc.)
  • PSEB registration (if any)

🏠 Property Owners

  • CNIC copy
  • Rental agreements & rent receipts
  • Property purchase / sale documents (if transacted)
  • Withholding tax challans (236C / 236K)

🌍 Non-Resident Pakistani

  • Annual income (worldwide)
  • Total assets held outside Pakistan
  • Foreign remittances sent to Pakistan
  • Annual expenses
  • Assets and liabilities
  • Any income generated within Pakistan (e.g. house rent)
